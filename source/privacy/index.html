Cookies

A cookie is a small file which asks permission to be stored by your web browser. Once accepted, the cookie file is transmitted back to our server as part of each new request and can be used to analyze web traffic and maintain information about your use of the application across individual requests so that our appliction can tailor its functionality and responses to your needs and preferences.

We use traffic log cookies (including third-party cookies) to identify which pages and resources are accessed by whom. This helps us to analyze data about web page traffic and improve our website. We only use this information for purposes of statistical analysis and only in aggregate.

Overall, cookies help us provide you with a better website, by enabling us to monitor which pages you find useful and which you do not. In no way does accepting a cookie allow BetterFBO or any affiliated third party to access your computer or any information about you, other than the data you choose to share with us.

You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser's privacy settings to decline cookies if you prefer. This may prevent you from taking full advantage of the website.
